The AI / Modern Angle

Luxury homes are becoming intelligent ecosystems — AI-controlled HVAC, automated security, smart water detection, and integrated energy management are standard in high-value properties. These systems prevent losses but also create new exposures: a smart home vulnerability can unlock every door, a compromised thermostat can cause pipe bursts, and an AI security system failure can leave a $10M property unprotected. Our high-value home coverage integrates with smart home platforms to provide real-time loss prevention monitoring — detecting water leaks, temperature anomalies, and security breaches before they become claims. Homeowners with connected systems receive premium credits that typically pay for the technology itself.
