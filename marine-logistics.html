The AI / Modern Angle

Global supply chains are increasingly managed by AI-driven logistics platforms that optimize routing, predict delays, and manage inventory autonomously. When these systems fail — misrouting high-value cargo, miscalculating cold-chain requirements, or failing to predict weather-related disruptions — the financial losses cascade through the entire supply network. Autonomous vessels and drone delivery are adding new liability dimensions that traditional marine policies weren't designed to cover. Our marine coverage integrates with AIS vessel tracking data, satellite weather feeds, and AI route optimization telemetry to provide real-time risk assessment and dynamic coverage adjustments.
