The AI / Modern Angle

Large construction projects now involve dozens of subcontractors, each bringing their own technology systems — autonomous equipment, AI-scheduled workflows, drone inspections, and digital twin models. When these systems interact, the liability chain becomes extraordinarily complex. Who is responsible when an AI scheduling system causes a crane operator and an autonomous concrete pourer to occupy the same space? Traditional wrap-up programs don't contemplate technology-driven interactions between trades. Our AI-enhanced wrap-up policies include automated subcontractor risk scoring, real-time safety compliance monitoring, and predictive incident analytics that reduce claims frequency while ensuring comprehensive coverage for every enrolled party.
