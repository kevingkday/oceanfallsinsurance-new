The AI / Modern Angle

The construction industry is being transformed by AI-powered project management, drone site inspections, BIM (Building Information Modeling), and robotic construction systems. These technologies introduce new risk dimensions — autonomous equipment malfunctions, AI scheduling errors that cascade into delay claims, and cyber vulnerabilities in smart building systems. Traditional builders risk policies don't account for technology-driven construction workflows. Our coverage integrates with IoT site sensors and drone inspection data to provide real-time risk monitoring and dynamic coverage adjustments throughout the construction lifecycle.
