The AI / Modern Angle

Niche businesses are rapidly adopting technology — fitness studios use AI for personalized training programs, daycares deploy facial recognition for child safety, and event venues rely on AI-powered crowd management. Each of these technologies creates new liability exposures that traditional general liability policies don't contemplate. When a fitness AI prescribes an exercise that causes injury, or a daycare's facial recognition system has a data breach, the coverage gap can be devastating. Our AI-enhanced underwriting uses industry-specific data models to accurately price risk for non-standard verticals, often resulting in better coverage at lower premiums than generic commercial packages.
