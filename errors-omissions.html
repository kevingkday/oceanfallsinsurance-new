The AI / Modern Angle

Traditional E&O policies were designed for human error — a consultant's bad advice or a developer's coding mistake. But when AI-powered software autonomously makes decisions that cause client losses, the liability landscape transforms fundamentally. An AI recommendation engine that triggers financial losses, a machine learning model that misclassifies medical images, or an automated system that corrupts data — these incidents blur the line between "error" and "product defect" in ways standard policies weren't built to handle. Our E&O coverage is specifically designed for the unique liability profile of AI-augmented professional services.
