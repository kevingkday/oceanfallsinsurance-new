The AI / Modern Angle

AI is transforming healthcare at velocity — radiology AI can now detect certain cancers with 94% accuracy, sometimes exceeding human radiologists. But when an AI diagnostic tool misses a malignancy, misclassifies a pathology slide, or provides an incorrect drug interaction warning, the malpractice implications are unprecedented. Who is liable — the physician who trusted the AI, the hospital that deployed it, or the vendor who built it? Traditional malpractice policies don't contemplate this shared liability model. Our coverage explicitly addresses AI-assisted misdiagnosis, algorithmic triage errors, and telemedicine platform failures, protecting clinicians who are adopting technology to improve patient outcomes.
