The AI / Modern Angle

The regulatory landscape for AI is fragmenting rapidly. The EU AI Act classifies AI systems into risk tiers with mandatory conformity assessments for high-risk applications. The US is introducing sector-specific guidelines through executive orders and agency rulemaking. Canada's AIDA, China's algorithmic regulations, and Brazil's AI framework each impose unique compliance burdens. Organizations deploying AI across borders face a compliance matrix that changes quarterly. Our policies dynamically adjust coverage scopes as new AI-specific laws are ratified, ensuring you're never caught off-guard by regulatory evolution.
