The AI / Modern Angle

The short-term rental economy relies on AI-powered dynamic pricing platforms, automated guest communications, smart locks, and noise monitoring devices. These technologies are essential for operating at scale — but they create coverage gaps that standard homeowners policies explicitly exclude. Most hosts don't realize their homeowners policy voids coverage the moment they accept a paying guest. Platform "host protection" programs are secondary coverage with significant exclusions. Our home sharing policies are primary coverage designed specifically for the STR business model, with dynamic premium adjustments based on your actual booking calendar — you only pay for coverage when your unit is active.
